Exploration Mission

Welcome to the Mars Exploration mission! Your task is to ride the rover and collect data on temperature and pressure readings from various locations on the planet's surface. Follow these instructions carefully to ensure the success of the mission: Board the rover and strap yourself in. Use the controls to navigate the rover to different locations on the planet's surface. Once you arrive at a location, use the onboard equipment to measure the temperature readings. Good luck, and safe travels on the Martian surface!
